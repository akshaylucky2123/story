Purpose:
The purpose of Mission Media U is to:
1 . Develop a scalable, sustainable online learning platform to distribute short-format curricula to missionaries and church leaders worldwide.
2. Offer courses that apply media, story and innovative technology directly to ministry.
3. Equip leaders to be better communicators, disciple-makers and church planters as they develop skills in using media, story and innovative technology in their mission.
